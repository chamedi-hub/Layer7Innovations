Risk Assessments & Security Strategy

What We Do

  • We provide a current state assessment based on best practice standards such as NIST and ISO cyber security frameworks.
  • A tailored strategy/roadmap will be developed for the client taking into consideration their organisational size and complexity.
  • Program Development, Security Metrics (measurement criteria) & Top Management Communications.

Compliance Services

What We Do

  • CyberCop provides PCI DSS & GDPR implementation assistance.
  • CyberCop is not a QSA but we can assist Organisations complete a gap assessment, remediation and deliver a SAQ as well as Quarterly PCI certified scans using Qualys.
  • Carrying out a GDPR readiness assessment, develop a GDPR road map and support in Implementing the road map.

Data Loss Prevention Services

What We Do

  • CyberCop can develop a Data Loss Prevention Strategy that includes People, Process and Technology requirements. We can also monitor DLP alerts

End Point Security Solutions

What We Do

  • Endpoint security solutions include working with Endpoint threat detection solutions such as Carbon Black, Crowdstrike, Trend Micro and Sophos. These solutions provide behavioural analysis to detect sophisticated attacks. CyberCop can implement and manage these solutions on behalf of our customer
